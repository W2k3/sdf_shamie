About Us

A safe society for a safer child.

At Shamie Destiny Foundation, we believe that every child deserves a chance to thrive, regardless of their circumstances. Our story began with a simple yet profound realization: that millions of children in Africa are struggling to survive, let alone reach their full potential.
Our mission is to provide critical support to vulnerable children in Africa, empowering them to break the cycle of poverty and achieve their dreams. We work tirelessly to:
- Provide access to quality education and healthcare
- Deliver emergency aid and relief to children in crisis
- Support community development initiatives that promote self-sufficiency
